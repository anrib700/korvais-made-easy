## Mridangam lessons

We have developed a [notation for representing Mridangam lessons][notation] ([PDF][notation-PDF])
which is concise, easy to learn and unambiguous. This method uses the duration
of the words in the lessons for its representation rather than the words
themselves.  This gives flexibility in playing the lessons. We welcome
suggestions from everyone so that we can improve this notation scheme. This
method has been constantly been evolving, and we shall openly consider to your
suggestions.

Please download the Thaniaavarthanam lessons given below, and [let us
know][contact] your views.

* [Ruparaka Thaalam, Trishra Nadai][3-6]
* [Aadhi Thaalam, Chatursra Mel Kaala Nadai][4-8]
* [Khanda Eka Thaalam, Khanda Nadai][5-5]
* [Mishra Eka Thaalam, Trishra Nadai][7-6]
* [Mishra Eka Thaalam, Misra Nadai][7-7]
* [Aadhi Thaalam, Trishra Nadai][8-6]
* [Aadhi Thaalam, Misra Nadai][8-7]
* [Aadhi Thaalam, Sankeerna Nadai][8-9]
* [Sankeerna Eka Thaalam, Sankeerna Nadai][9-9] [audio][99mp3] {{99mp3}}
* [Trishra Mukhi Thaalam, Thrishra Nadai][9.5-6]
* [Mishra Jampa Thaalam, Sankeerna Nadai][10-9]
